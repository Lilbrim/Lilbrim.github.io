Cats fun fact

Did you know that cats have five toes on their front paws, but only four toes on their back paws?
This is because cats' front paws are designed for precision, providing them with better grip and dexterity.
The extra toe, known as the "dewclaw," is located higher up on the leg and doesn't touch the ground. In contrast,
their back paws have a more streamlined structure, allowing them to generate power and speed when they run and jump.
This unique feature adds to the agility and grace that cats are known for!.
